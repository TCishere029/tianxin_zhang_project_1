Schizophrenia

Children's Mental Health Crisis Line: 651-266-7878

Adult Mental Health Crisis Line: 651-266-7900

Schizophrenia is a complex mental disorder that affects thinking, behavior, and emotions. It is characterized by delusions, hallucinations, disorganized speech, and impaired functioning. Delusions are false beliefs, and hallucinations involve perceiving things that aren't real. Disorganized thinking and abnormal behavior are also symptoms. Negative symptoms, like reduced function and lack of emotion or interest, can occur. Schizophrenia greatly affects cognitive, social, and emotional functioning, impacting overall well-being and quality of life. Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial for managing symptoms and improving outcomes.
