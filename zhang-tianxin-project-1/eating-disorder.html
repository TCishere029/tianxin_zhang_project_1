Eating Disorder

Children's Mental Health Crisis Line: 651-266-7878

Adult Mental Health Crisis Line: 651-266-7900

Eating disorders are complex mental health conditions characterized by abnormal eating habits, extreme concerns about body weight or shape, and intense emotions related to food and body image. These disorders, which include anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, and binge-eating disorder, can lead to serious physical and psychological complications. The root causes are multifaceted, encompassing genetic, environmental, psychological, and sociocultural factors. While societal pressures and idealized body images often play a role, the deeper nuances of these disorders vary among individuals. Treatment typically involves a combination of nutritional counseling, psychotherapy, and medical care to address both the psychological and physical aspects of the condition.
